A New Sound

Laufey's music is a blend of the jazz, pop, and classical melodies found in musical culture. With her rich alto voice and solemn yet catchy melodies, her songs are a mix of authentic feeling and intention. The cocktail of familiar gnres of music allow it to feel nostalgic and cozy, but with twists and lyrics that allow it to stand out from ofthr artists of the era.
